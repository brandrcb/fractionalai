Questions

Questions Buckhead firms ask us

How do you handle confidentiality for a law firm or financial advisory practice?

Confidentiality is the first conversation, not an afterthought. We work exclusively within enterprise-grade platforms that do not train on your data. Each agent is scoped to access only what its specific task requires — an intake agent handles intake; it doesn't touch billing records. We work within your existing NDAs, BAAs where applicable, and client confidentiality agreements. Every data touchpoint is documented before deployment.

What does the white-glove setup process look like?

We begin with a detailed review of your intake workflow, your communication standards, and your brand voice — including actual client correspondence samples to calibrate tone. Nothing reaches a real client until you've reviewed and approved it during testing. The rollout includes a dedicated training session for your team, and we remain available for the first 30 days post-launch to tune anything that needs adjustment.

Can agents match our firm's brand voice and communication standards?

Yes — and that calibration is built into our process, not added as an option. We train agents on your real templates, actual communication samples (redacted as appropriate), and explicit tone guidelines you provide. A Buckhead wealth advisory firm writes differently than a Kennesaw contractor, and the agents we build for you will reflect that. The goal is responses that your clients cannot distinguish from your best staff member's writing — because that's the standard your reputation requires.
