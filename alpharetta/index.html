Questions

Questions Alpharetta business owners ask us

How is this different from ChatGPT — we already use that?

ChatGPT answers questions when you ask them. An AI agent takes actions on a schedule or in response to triggers — without you prompting it. When a new lead hits your CRM, an agent reads it, writes a personalized follow-up in your brand voice, sends it, and logs the activity — automatically. Prompting is a skill. The businesses pulling ahead are building systems.

Do agents integrate with our CRM and existing tools?

Yes — that's the whole point. We build integrations with HubSpot, Salesforce, GoHighLevel, Pipedrive, and most major CRMs. Agents also connect to your calendar, email, proposal tools (PandaDoc, Proposify), and any platform with an API or Zapier connection. Before we scope the engagement, we confirm your exact stack and how the integrations will work.

How do you handle data safety for a professional services firm?

We work exclusively within enterprise-grade AI platforms that do not train on your data. Each agent is scoped to access only the data its specific task requires — a follow-up agent sees leads, not your full client database. We document every data touchpoint before deployment and can work within your existing MSAs and NDAs. Data handling is a first conversation, not an afterthought.
